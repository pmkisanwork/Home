Refund & Cancellation Policy (धनवापसी और रद्दीकरण नीति)

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Overview (सारांश)

At All Work Online, we are committed to providing reliable and transparent services. This policy explains our refund and cancellation process for all transactions made through our platform.

हम पारदर्शी और भरोसेमंद सेवाएं देने के लिए प्रतिबद्ध हैं।

2. Cancellation Policy (रद्दीकरण नीति)

  • Service requests can be cancelled within 24 hours of booking, provided the service has not been initiated.
  • Once a service has been processed or submitted to government portals, cancellation is not possible.
  • To cancel a request, contact us within 24 hours at our helpline or email.
  • सेवा शुरू होने के बाद रद्दीकरण संभव नहीं है।

3. Refund Eligibility (धनवापसी पात्रता)

Refunds will be considered in the following cases:

  • Duplicate payment made by the user.
  • Payment deducted but service not initiated due to technical error.
  • Service cancelled within the allowed 24-hour window.
⚠️ Note: Most government-related services (PM KISAN, eKYC, etc.) are free of charge. Payments, if any, are only for value-added assistance services.

4. Non-Refundable Cases (धनवापसी नहीं होगी)

  • Service has already been delivered or submitted.
  • Cancellation requested after 24 hours of payment.
  • Incorrect information provided by the user causing service failure.
  • Government portal rejection due to user's own documentation issues.
  • Transaction/payment gateway charges are non-refundable.

5. Refund Process (धनवापसी प्रक्रिया)

  • Submit refund request via email or helpline within 24 hours.
  • Provide your transaction ID, registered mobile number, and reason for refund.
  • Refund requests will be reviewed within 5–7 business days.
  • Approved refunds will be credited to the original payment source within 7–10 business days.
