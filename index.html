Sabhi common questions ke jawab yahan milenge

🌾 PM KISAN registration ke liye kya documents chahiye? ▼
Aadhaar Card, Bank Passbook, Khasra/Khatauni (Zameen ka kagaz), aur Mobile Number. Yeh sab documents lekar aayein — hum poora process handle kar lete hain.
🔐 eKYC kaise hoti hai aur kitna time lagta hai? ▼
OTP Based eKYC sirf 5-10 minute mein ho jaati hai. Aapke Aadhaar se linked mobile number par OTP aata hai. Biometric eKYC ke liye hamara CSC center visit karein — yeh bhi 15 minute ka kaam hai.
💰 PM KISAN ki 22nd installment kab aayegi? ▼
PM Modi ji 22nd installment 13 March 2026 ko Guwahati, Assam se release karenge. Paisa directly aapke bank account mein aayega. Status check karne ke liye humse contact karein.
📋 Farmer Registry mein naam kaise add karwayein? ▼
Farmer Registry mein registration ke liye Aadhaar, zameen ki nakal aur bank details chahiye. Hum aapka form bharte hain aur verification bhi karwate hain. Poora process 2-3 din mein complete hota hai.
📞 Kya ghar baithe service milti hai? ▼
Haan! Bahut saari services online/phone se ho jaati hain. Call ya WhatsApp karein 8461246946 par — hum aapko guide karenge. Zaroorat padne par home visit bhi arrange ki ja sakti hai.
